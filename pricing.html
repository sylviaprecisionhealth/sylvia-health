Pricing FAQ

Common questions about pricing.

Is there a free trial available? +
We are currently in early access. Contact us to discuss trial options for individual users and clinicians interested in piloting Sylvia.
Can I switch plans after starting? +
Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time. Your assessment data and Personalized Recovery Roadmap carry forward to any plan.
Does insurance cover Sylvia? +
We are working to establish insurance coverage pathways. Currently Sylvia is a self-pay service. We are committed to making precision eating disorder care as accessible as possible and are actively exploring coverage options.
Is there a discount for clinicians or students? +
We offer special pricing for training clinicians, graduate students in clinical programs, and nonprofit treatment providers. Contact us to learn more.
