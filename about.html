Contact us
Who is Sylvia designed for? +
Sylvia is designed for individuals with eating disorders or disordered eating, as well as clinicians, treatment centers, and public health agencies who treat eating disorders.
Is my personal health data kept private and secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Sylvia is built with clinical-grade data security standards and is designed to be HIPAA-compliant. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties.
How long does the assessment take? +
The assessment phase takes approximately two weeks. During this time, users complete brief smartphone surveys multiple times per day. Each check-in takes only a few minutes.
Can I share my results with my clinician? +
Yes. Sylvia is designed to support clinical integration. You can share your Roadmap and assessment results securely with your existing care team.
Do I need a wearable device to use Sylvia? +
No. Sylvia works entirely through your smartphone. You do not need any wearable device, external sensor, or additional hardware.
How can organizations implement Sylvia? +
Sylvia offers full program implementation support for treatment centers, community mental health centers, and public health agencies. Contact us to discuss implementation for your organization.
