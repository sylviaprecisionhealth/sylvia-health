How It Works

Driving measurable recovery results.

Sylvia uses real-time mobile monitoring and advanced data analytics to identify the precise mechanisms driving each individual's eating disorder.

Real-Time Mobile Monitoring

Users complete brief smartphone assessments multiple times daily, capturing eating behaviors, thoughts, emotions, and mental health symptoms in real time over two weeks.

Evidence-Based Recovery

Results connect directly to evidence-based exercises from The Personalized Recovery Workbook for Eating Disorders, providing targeted, clinically-grounded guidance.

Precision Analytics

Idiographic network modeling algorithms analyze your data to identify the most central symptoms driving your disorder, creating a truly personalized recovery map.

Personalized Recovery Roadmap

A data-generated plan that prioritizes the most impactful targets for change, specific to each individual's symptom profile, not a generic protocol.

FAQ

Need Help? We're here.

Everything you need to know about Sylvia and how it works.

Who is Sylvia designed for? +
Sylvia is designed for individuals with eating disorders or disordered eating, as well as clinicians, treatment centers, and public health agencies who treat eating disorders. Our tiered model serves everyone from individuals seeking self-guided support to clinical practices looking to implement personalized treatment at scale.
How long does the assessment take? +
The assessment phase takes approximately two weeks. During this time, users complete brief smartphone surveys multiple times per day, capturing real-time data on eating behaviors, thoughts, emotions, and related symptoms. These brief check-ins typically take just a few minutes each.
Is my personal health data kept private and secure? +
Yes. Your health data is kept strictly private and secure. Sylvia is built with clinical-grade data security standards. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties, and all data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
What is a Personalized Recovery Roadmap? +
A Personalized Recovery Roadmap is a data-generated, individualized plan that identifies the most central symptoms driving your eating disorder and prioritizes the most impactful targets for change. It is paired with evidence-based exercises from The Personalized Recovery Workbook for Eating Disorders.
Can clinicians use Sylvia with their patients? +
Yes. Sylvia offers a Clinician-Integrated Care tier specifically for practitioners. Clinicians can receive personalized treatment plans for their patients, access optional psychologist consultation, and implement the Roadmap across an entire patient population.
What makes Sylvia different from other mental health apps? +
Sylvia uses idiographic network modeling, an advanced algorithm that analyzes each individual's symptom patterns rather than applying population averages. This means your recovery plan is built from your own data, not a generic template. It is precision medicine applied to eating disorder treatment.
